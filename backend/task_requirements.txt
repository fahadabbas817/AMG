SCOPE OF WORK (SOW) – AMG Vendor Database, Payments &amp; Tracking System 


Project Overview

This project aims to build an online platform where AMG can manage its vendors, revenue reports, payments, and statistics, and where vendors can securely log in to view their earnings and performance from multiple platforms.
The system will automate revenue calculations, commission deductions, and integrate selected data with QuickBooks Online.


2. Project Goals

Centralized Vendor DatabaseStore all vendor information, payout details, platform splits, and related data in one place for AMG internal use.


Vendor PortalGive vendors a secure login where they can see their monthly revenues, platform stats, pending payments, and payout history.


Automated Revenue CalculationsBased on uploaded reports, automatically calculate revenues, vendor earnings, AMG’s commission, and total dues.


QuickBooks Online Integration Sync:

Vendor records
Payment dates &amp; references
Revenue amounts 
Between AMG system ↔ QuickBooks Online.

AMG VENDOR DATABASE_PAYMENTS &amp; TRACKING – DEVELOPMENT V2

Objective: To create an online tracking system that our partners (Vendors) could login to and see their revenues earned and video statistics generated from multiple platforms. 
Goals: 
Create a centralized database which provides a reference point for all data associated with a vendor. Associated data would be company details, banking/payout information, sub-studios, platforms contracted and payout split per platform. This would be for AMG reference.
To create an online, password-protected, solution for our partners (Vendors) to login and track the money they are owed from each platform. 
Auto-calculate AMG’s Vendor/Platform specific commission.

Important Points to Consider in Development:
UI should be extremely easy and basic on Vendor side. Functionality limited to seeing statistics per platform, per month, amount pending and payment dates for past revenues. 
AMG Admin UI should also be extremely easy to navigate with editable flexibility in most fields. 
The ability to cross-populate some of these details to our Quickbooks accounting system, mainly: 1) on file Vendor details; Quickbooks 🡪➡️ to Vendor Database. 3) Vendor Payment Dates w/ References Quickbooks payout🡪➡️ to Vendor Database. 4) Revenues owed to Vendors based on Reports uploaded 🡪➡️ Quickbooks.
Quickbooks has this feature “Sync to spreadsheets” that might be useful. Here’s a YoutTube Video about it https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IZsXw9UmLRE&amp;t=221s
